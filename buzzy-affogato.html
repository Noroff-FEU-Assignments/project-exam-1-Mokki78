Buzzy Affogato

Ingredients:

  • 45m Elephant Gin Coffee Liqueur
  • 15ml hazelnut liqueur
  • 30ml espresso
  • 1 scoop of vanilla ice cream
  • Add the coffee liqueur, hazelnut liqueur and espresso to a shaker with ice and shake well.
  • 4 oz dark rum, divided

How to:

Add the coffee liqueur, hazelnut liqueur and espresso to a shaker with ice and shake well. Strain into a glass over the vanilla ice cream. Enjoy!

Fun Fact:

Affogato is a good old classic Italian dessert. A cup of Affogato is as simple as it gets. The main ingredients behind an excellent serving of Affogato only include a scoop of rich, sweet, and milky vanilla gelato bathed in a shot of strong, hot espresso. 

Affegato is translated to "drown" in Italian.

Perfect for:

This is the perfect dessert for autumn and winter dinners.
