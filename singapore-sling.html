Singapore Sling

Ingredients:

  • 25ml dry gin
  • 25ml cherry brandy
  • 25ml Benedictine
  • handful of ice, to serve
  • few drops Angostura bitters
  • 50ml pineapple juice
  • 25ml lime juice
  • sparkling water to top up
  • 1 thin slice of fresh pineapple (optional) 
  • cocktail cherry

How to:

  1. Add gin, cherry brandy and Benedictine into a mixing glass or jug.
  2. Add ice cubes and the angostura and stir until the outside of the glass is cold.
  3. Pour the mix into tall glasses and add the pineapple juice and lime juice and mix gently.
  4. Last step is to add the sparkling water and garnish, and the drinks are ready to be enjoyed.

Fun Fact:

The "Original" Singapore Sling cocktail was created sometime between 1910 & 1915 by barman Ngiam Tong Boon at the Raffles Hotel in Singapore

No two Singapore Sling recipes documented over the past 100 years or so are alike.

Perfect for:

This is my go-to drink in summer time and for summer parties.
