Christmas Margarita

Ingredients:

  • 3 ounces cranberry juice
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • 1 ounce triple sec or orange liqueur
  • 2 ounces tequila
  • garnish: lime slice, orange slice, cranberries, rosemary

How to:

  1. Rim a cocktail glass with either salt or sugar( I recommend sugar)
  2. Add ingredients to your cocktail shaker and mix while you listen to your favorite Christmas song.
  3. Strain into your prepared glass.
  4. Add your chosen garnish.

Fun Fact:

According to the Cheers On-Preminse Handbook, 2008, Margaritas is the most popular drink in America, and there is supposedly consumed 185,000 margaritas per hour in America.

There is a national Margarita day celebratedevery year on February 22nd by thousands of Americans and Mexican restaurants around the country.

Perfect for:

This drink is both delicious and with its gorgeous red color also looks christmasy and tasty, so perfect at every christmas party and christmas dinner. Yes, even a perfect drink for christmas eve.(if you are not celebrating with kids.)
