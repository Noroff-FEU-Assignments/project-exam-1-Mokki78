Green park

Ingredients:

  • 6 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 2/3 shot Old Tom gin
  • 1 shot lime juice( freshly squeezed)
  • 1/2 shot sugar syrup (2 sugar to 1 water)
  • 3 dash celery bitter
  • 1/2 shot pastereurised egg whites

How to:

Lightly muddle basil in base of shaker, and add the rest of the ingredients. Shake with ice and strain back into shaker. Dry shake(without ice) and fine strain into chilled glass(es.)

Fun Fact:

Green park is also the name of a park in London, stretching north and west from Buckingham Palace.

One serving of the drink only contain 166 calories.

Perfect for:

Dinner with the girls, or as a surprise drink for you sister or mum.
