Healthy Grapefruit Paloma Cocktail (non-alcoholic)

Ingredients:

  • Grape fruit
  • Lime juice
  • Maple syrup
  • Sparkling water

How to:

  1. Add the juice and maple syrup into a longdrink glass and stir well.
  2. Add the ice to the mix.
  3. Top it off with the sparkling water, and ad the garnish( a slice of lime or peace of grapefruit)

Fun Fact:

The difference between a cocktail and mocktail is that cocktail is a drink that mixes either one type of alcohol with juices, or mixes multiple alcoholic beverages with juices. A mocktail is any mixed drink that does not have alcohol!
