Brandy Alexander

Ingredients:

  • 1 ounce brandy
  • 1 ounce dark crème de cacao liqueur
  • 1 ounce cream
  • Freshly grated nutmeg, for garnish

How to:

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Add the brandy, crème de cacao, the cream and Ince cubes to a shake and shake well.
  3. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  4. Decorate the drink with the dusting of nutmeg.Enjoy!

Fun Fact:

Troy Alexander purportedly came up with the drink while working as a bartender at famed New York City restaurant Rector's.ian.

Beatles fans will be quick to note that the Brandy Alexander was John Lennon's favorite cocktail.

Perfect for:

Late night night-caps, after dinner drink instead of a dessert or for the Christmas party.
